Learn a little about me

Hi, I'm Albina Soto. My journey into postpartum care began at San Jose State University, where I studied Child Development. For the past decade, I've dedicated my career to the "under-five" journey-spending years in both formal educational settings and providing personalized childcare. I've seen firsthand the magic of a child's discovery, but I've also seen how much parents need a "village" of their own. While my years as a nanny have been dedicated to nurturing the individual needs of children, my transition into postpartum care has a new, vital objective. At Olive Branch Baby Care, I believe that the best way to ensure a child's future success is to nurture and equip the parents first. By providing you with evidence-based solutions, a listening ear, and practical support during the fourth trimester, I help create a stable, peaceful environment. My goal is to empower you with the confidence and tools you need today, so your children can thrive in the years to come.

  • Certified Adult & Pediatric First Aid / CPR / AED

  • All vaccinations up to date
