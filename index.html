Let's Build Your Village!

My mission at Olive Branch Baby Care is to extend a helping hand during one of life's most profound transitions. I believe that the early weeks of parenthood shouldn't be defined by stress, but by supported growth. My objective is to ensure your transition is as smooth as possible by providing evidence-based information and hands-on care tailored to your family's unique needs. Whether I am offering a listening ear for your birth story, troubleshooting newborn sleep, or simply keeping your home running smoothly while you rest, I am there to bridge the gap between 'surviving' and 'thriving.' I don't just care for your baby; I can offer collaborative guidance that will support your parenting intuition.

About Me

With a background in Child Development from SJSU and a decade of experience in both formal education and private childcare, I understand that the first few weeks of life are a sacred time of discovery. My transition into postpartum care was born from a desire to support the most intimate needs of a growing family. I'm here to offer the solutions, education, and peace of mind you deserve.
